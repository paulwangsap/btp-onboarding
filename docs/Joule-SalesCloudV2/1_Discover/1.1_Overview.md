## SAP Joule for Sales Cloud V2 Overview

Joule with SAP Sales Cloud Version 2 enable sales teams to have quality customer interactions, empowered with intelligent recommendations to help win deals leveraging its generative AI capabilities. It aims to understand your requests, retrieve information, and complete your tasks in a conversational way, thus sales representatives can easily interact with the system and improve productivity.SAP Joule for Sales Cloud V2 introduces generative AI capabilities that enhance the user experience across all sales and service features. Joule empowers buyers and category managers to interact with Ariba processes and content through natural language — driving faster decisions, improved compliance, and greater operational efficiency. In this mission, you’ll learn how to prepare your Ariba system, activate Joule, and validate successful integration
